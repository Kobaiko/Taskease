Privacy Policy

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@taskease.com.
